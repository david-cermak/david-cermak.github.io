ICYMI: AirSnitch🛜 cool toolkit to check your Wi-Fi

👉 https://github.com/vanhoefm/airsnitch
This is a research toolkit that shows how modern Wi-Fi protections can be bypassed in practice: Not about cracking WPA, but looking into architectural weaknesses in APs/forwarding and encryption.
Interestingly this can:
* Bypass client isolation and injects frames into encrypted Wi-Fi
* Run MitM inside the same WLAN
