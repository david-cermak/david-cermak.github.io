Mozilla fixed 423 Firefox security bugs in April (~20x their monthly average), but... is it all about Mythos?

The Register published an interesting article on this topic
🔗 https://www.theregister.com/security/2026/05/08/mozilla-says-ai-helped-squash-423-firefox-security-bugs/5235438

More on his blog🔗https://www.flyingpenguin.com/could-mozilla-security-hot-air-fill-mythos-sails/
