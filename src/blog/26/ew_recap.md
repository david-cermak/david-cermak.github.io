#EW26: 💬What people talked about

After a few busy days at Embedded World in Nuremberg, I wanted to share the topics that kept coming up in conversations -- from my personal perspective.

There's already a nice article which captured the big picture really well:
*10 Strategic Trends Driving Embedded Systems*
🔗https://lnkd.in/dmV742Ri

My personal list of topics is (almost) in line with those trends:
1️⃣ *AI everywhere*
From coding tools and agentic pipelines to Edge AI and NN frameworks running on devices.
2️⃣ *Zephyr *& *Rust*
Zephyr RTOS ecosystem is expanding quickly and gaining strong momentum; Rust is increasingly seen as a viable option for embedded development.
3️⃣ *CRA *& *security*
Many teams preparing for upcoming Cyber Resilience Act milestones.
4️⃣ *Arduino as a platform*
More engineers are comfortable treating Arduino not just as a hobby ecosystem but as a serious development platform; especially with the new Qualcomm + Arduino initiatives.
5️⃣ *Mesh *& *industrial IoT*
A frequent topic in my conversations, even though it didn’t make the “top trends” list.

Thanks to everyone who stopped by for the great discussions; and big thanks to the Espressif and M5Stack teams👏
